# Data description

Variable name
- choice: the choice variable 0=car, 1=rail
- business_trip: the trip was a business trip 1=yes, 0=no
- age41+: the traveller is over 41 years old 1=yes, 0=no
- unemployed: the traveller is unemployed 1=yes, 0=no
- female: the traveller is female 1=yes, 0=no
- npersons: number of persons travelling together
- train_cost: alternative specific variable cost for train choice
- train_tt: alternative specific variable travel time for train choice
- train_xfers: alternative specific variable number of transfers for train choice
- train_egress_walk: alternative specific variable dummy traveller walked from the train station 1=yes, 0=no
- train_ingress_walk: alternative specific variable dummy traveller walked to the train station 1=yes, 0=no
- train_1st_class: alternative specific variable dummy traveller used 1st class
- car_cost: alternative specific variable cost for car choice
- car_tt: alternative specific variable travel time for car choice
- car_walk_time: alternative specific variable total walking time parking/destination
- free_parking: alternative specific variable dummy for free parking 1=yes, 0=no
- flex_arrival_time: dummy if arrival time is flexible for traveller 1=yes, 0=no
